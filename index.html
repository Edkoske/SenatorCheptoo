About Senator Cheptoo

A principled public servant with a record of community leadership—focused on practical solutions and honest stewardship.

Background

Senator Cheptoo is a daughter of Kericho County, inspired by the resilience of farmers, the ambition of youth, and the strength of women’s groups. She believes leadership must be close to the people—present, respectful, and accountable.

  • Deep grassroots connection across wards
  • Strong collaboration with community leaders
  • Commitment to transparent public service

Key Agenda / Manifesto

Clear commitments you can measure—focused on livelihoods, services, and ethical leadership.

Jobs & opportunity

  • County-wide skills and mentorship programs for youth
  • Support for MSMEs and small traders through training and linkages
  • Promote fair procurement opportunities for local enterprises

Women & families

  • Strengthen women groups with capacity building and market access
  • Champion safer public spaces and community-based support systems
  • Advocate for inclusive development for persons with disabilities

Farmers & agriculture

  • Push for fair tea prices, timely payments, and transparent deductions
  • Support diversification and value addition to increase incomes
  • Back climate-smart agriculture and water solutions for farmers

Education & health

  • Oversight for fair, efficient funding for health facilities and schools
  • Support bursary transparency and merit-based access for needy students
  • Promote youth mental health and community health outreach programs

Governance

  • Zero tolerance for corruption—publish commitments and progress updates
  • Stronger citizen participation and open forums across wards
  • Champion oversight to ensure projects deliver real value
